What is e-Gold Western Union service?

We provide e-Gold Western Union service to redeem or fund e-Gold account via Western Union. You can buy e-Gold via Western Union or sell e-Gold and receive funds via Western Union. There is a similar service for c-Gold, Pecunix, Liberty Reserve, WebMoney and others.

How to fund e-currency account via Western Union:

1. Fill out the buy e-currency form.
2. Find a Western Union location nearest to you and send money to us.
3. Come back to our website and enter the Western Union transfer information.
4. We will pick up the transfer shortly thereafter and fund your e-Gold, Pecunix, Liberty Reserve, c-Gold or WebMoney account within the specified timeframe.

How to redeem e-currency account and receive funds via Western Union:

1. Fill out the sell e-currency form.
2. Confirm the order and submit your payment by e-Gold, c-Gold, Pecunix, Liberty Reserve or WebMoney.
3. We will send the Western Union transfer and email you the details you need to pick up the transfer.
4. Find a Western Union location nearest to you and pickup money.
